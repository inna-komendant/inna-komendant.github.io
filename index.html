I`m a print and clothing designer educated at School of Fine Art and Design Kherson. In 2013 I have been working as home textile designer, from 2014 to 2017 - as clothing designer and product developer focused on different ways of printing textiles for baby clothes. Now i study textile technologies at Technical University of Liberec.


I like to mix technologies, layer on layer, make collages, but still keep simplicity and sensitivity in my creations. I mainly find my inspiration in nature and geometry.
